# Code of Conduct

DuoAsk should be a useful, respectful place for people who are building, testing, documenting, and maintaining local-first AI tooling.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and specific.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing provider behavior that may differ by account, region, or UI rollout.
- Keep issue reports focused on reproducible behavior.
- Remove secrets, cookies, prompt history, account details, and private screenshots before posting logs or images.
- Respect provider terms, rate limits, login flows, CAPTCHA, and manual verification.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Posting another person's private data, credentials, screenshots, or session material.
- Asking contributors to bypass login, CAPTCHA, provider verification, usage limits, or provider terms.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers have asked to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from the project spaces.

If you need to report a concern, contact the maintainer privately or use GitHub's reporting tools where appropriate.
